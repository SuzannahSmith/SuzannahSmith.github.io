Work Experience

Suzannah's first official job was working at a day camp for kids in Quebec. This was part of the YMCA's Summer Work Student Exchange six-week program. It was a great opportunity to explore Quebec, practice French, and earn a good paycheck.

The summer after she graduated from high school she began working at a Tim Hortons in Fredericton. She continued working there throughout her first year of university, but then moved on to the Computer Science co-op program.

Her first Computer Science internship was at Professional Quality Assurance (PQA) in Fredericton. She worked there from May to August 2015. Some of her tasks included manually testing websties, testing the content of video-based courses, and writing detailed reviews and bug reports.
